Ferdie Franz

Field Guide & Pest Control Specialist

Ferdie Franz is a passionate, reliable and hardworking field guide, FGASA Level 1 qualified with hands-on experience in a 4-star lodge environment. Skilled in tracking, birding and bush interpretation, and rifle competent, he brings a lifetime of farm and maintenance experience to every job. Fit, dependable and conservation-minded, Ferdie is always ready to work hard and make a difference.

Pest Control

From pigeons, doves and guinea fowl to jackal, caracal, baboon and warthog — Ferdie offers professional pest control services for farmers, protecting crops, livestock and farm environments. With rifle competency, sharp bush skills and a deep understanding of animal behaviour, every problem animal is handled safely, responsibly and effectively. Problem animals, big problems — solved.
